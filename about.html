AboutRedington High School Football Booster Club

Redington High School Football Booster Club was founded to address gaps in community support where public systems cannot reach fast enough. Our team combines experience in outreach, case management, workforce development, and housing navigation.

We believe that lasting change happens when relief, employment, and housing efforts are tied together through clear data and verified outcomes. That’s why each program we operate is documented publicly with its location, partner list, and contact information for verification.

Core Principles

Transparency

Every service area and spending report is made available for partner review. Agencies can easily verify our EIN 10240995 and public address 3730 N Fireweed Fields Dr., Wasilla, AK 99623.

Accountability

We track outcomes beyond simple headcounts — documenting engagement, employment, and stability over time.

Local Partnership

We don’t work alone. Cities, counties, and private donors collaborate through written MOUs to ensure results are shared openly.
